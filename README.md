# MarketPorter

**Structured order management for proxy buying services.**

---

## What is MarketPorter?

MarketPorter is a workflow system built for proxy buying — known in some regions as *jastip*. It replaces the chaos of chat-based ordering with a structured, trackable ledger for every purchase request.

Proxy buyers act as intermediaries between customers and online marketplaces. A customer finds a product on a Japanese marketplace like **Mercari** or **PayPay Flea Market**, sends the link to a proxy buyer, and asks them to purchase it on their behalf. Traditionally, this entire process happens inside messaging apps — and that's where things break down.

Links get buried in conversations. Prices are unclear. Orders are forgotten. Proof of purchase and shipping details are scattered across threads with no central record.

MarketPorter solves this by turning every product link into a formal, documented order.

---

## How It Works

When a customer submits a product link, the system reads the page and extracts key product information:

- **Product title**
- **Price**
- **Image**
- **Seller information**

The link is then converted into a structured order record inside the system. No more digging through chat history — every request becomes a tracked transaction from the moment it's submitted.

---

## Order Lifecycle

Each order follows a clear, step-by-step lifecycle:

| Stage | Description |
|---|---|
| **Requested** | Customer submits a product link. The system extracts product details and creates the order. |
| **Confirmed** | Proxy buyer reviews the request and confirms the order with the customer. |
| **Purchased** | The item has been bought from the marketplace. Proof of purchase is attached. |
| **Arrived** | The item arrives at the proxy buyer's location. |
| **Shipped** | The item is shipped to the customer. Tracking information is recorded. |
| **Completed** | The customer receives the item. The transaction is closed. |

The proxy buyer documents every step. The system stores product details, price calculations, service fees, purchase screenshots, invoices, and shipping tracking numbers — all attached to the order.

---

## What MarketPorter Is Not

MarketPorter does **not** automatically purchase items or execute payments. The actual purchase is still done manually by the operator. The system's purpose is to **organize, document, and track** the entire proxy buying process — not to automate it.

---

## Why It Matters

Without a system like this, proxy buying relies on memory, message search, and manual bookkeeping. Orders slip through the cracks. Customers ask for updates and the proxy buyer has to scroll through weeks of messages to find a screenshot or a tracking number.

MarketPorter replaces that with a single source of truth. Every request becomes a documented transaction with a full history. Both the customer and the proxy buyer get clarity — what was ordered, what it cost, where it is, and what happens next.

**One link in. One structured order out. Every step recorded.**
